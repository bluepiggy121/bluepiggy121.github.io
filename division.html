Division 30 South

Division 30 South is one of the seven divisions making up Region 3, also known as the Rainbow Region. of the California-Nevada-Hawaii District of Key Club International. Our division is comprised of 14 schools, including Oxford Academy Key Club. First formed in the year 1996, the divisional mascot of D30S has always been the Sun where we shine while serving the community. For the 20+ years since it came to be, D30S has continued to shine, grow, and learn as it focuses on service and keeps the power of the Sun alive.

The 14 schools that make up Division 30 South are Anaheim, Cypress, Katella, Kennedy, Loara, Los Alamitos, Magnolia, Oxford, Savanna, Servite, Western, and Whitney. As a member of Oxford Academy Key Club, you also become a member of Division 30 South Key Club! The division frequently holds division-wide events such as DCMs, service projects, and socials. On occasion, D30S also meets up with other divisions for bonding activities, training conferences, and other fun things!

The division is also run by the Division Leadership Team, a board of officers in which the Lieutenant Governor takes charge of. If you ever have questions regarding the division, remember to reach out to the DLT for more information! Together, Division 30 South shines strong!
