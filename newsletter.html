OAKC Dues Payment

All Key Clubbers must pay a $12 fee in order to become a dues-paid member. Pay using the ASB webstore, which is in our LinkTree under the dues payment tab. Here are the steps for payment: 1) Register for an ASB Webstore account. 2) Select Key Club under product categories. 3) Add to cart and then checkout. Also, make sure to complete the member application form in order to become a dues-paid member.
