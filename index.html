OAKC Mental Health Activity: Self-Care Strategy

September is National Suicide Prevention Month, and OAKC is encouraging volunteers to write notes to yourselves as acts of self-care! The activity is meant to help yourself grow and reflect on your future actions. Instead of writing positive affirmations, challenge yourself by turning the statements into questions. Due September 20
