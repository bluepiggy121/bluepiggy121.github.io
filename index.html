What's New

Color-A-Smile

Volunteers will color in order to help spread smiles! Color-A-Smile is a nonprofit organization that distributes cheerful drawing to anyone in need of a smile. Print out however many coloring pages as you'd like from the link, color it with crayons or markers, and mail it to the address listed in the form!

  • Due Sept 29

Beanbeanbean

Volunteers will answer questions to earn "beans" for every right answer using the website https://beanbeanbean.com. Screenshot the final page of the beans you earned and submit it to the Google Form!

  • Due Sept 29

Charity Miles

Volunteers will earn money for charity for every mile walked/ran through an app called Charity Miles. Each mile will directly be converted into donations for a charity of your choice.

  • Due Sept 29

OAKC Mental Health Activity: Self-Care Strategy

September is National Suicide Prevention Month, and OAKC is encouraging volunteers to write notes to yourselves as acts of self-care! The activity is meant to help yourself grow and reflect on your future actions. Instead of writing positive affirmations, challenge yourself by turning the statements into questions.

  • Due Sept 20

New OAKC Remind!

As we progress more into the term and have more online service events, OAKC now has a Remind for all members to join! Use the class code @oakc2020 and join through the app, or text the code to the number 81010.
