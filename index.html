What's New

Color-A-Smile

Volunteers will color in order to help spread smiles! Color-A-Smile is a nonprofit organization that distributes cheerful drawing to anyone in need of a smile. Print out however many coloring pages as you'd like from the link, color it with crayons or markers, and mail it to the address listed in the form!

Fotition

Volunteers will earn money for charity and spread awareness for good causes through sharing photos with the app Fotition. The steps in order to complete the activity and earn hours are listed in the Google form. Submit your screenshot of the profile screen in the Google form!

  • More

Love for the Elderly

Volunteers will write handwritten letters to the elderly to fight loneliness and help them feel loved! Make sure to be kind and thoughtful in your letters, and embrace your creativity!

  • More

Charity Miles

Volunteers will earn money for charity for every mile walked/ran through an app called Charity Miles. Each mile will directly be converted into donations for a charity of your choice.
