What's New

Shave The Day

Download the game, "Shave the Day," to help St. Baldrick's Foundation raise money for childhood cancer research! All you have to do is play the game and try to shave as many heads as possible!

  • Due Oct 13

Rice Race

Race to donate on this mobile game! For every grain of rice you collect in the game, Rice Race donates one to Foodbank. These donations will help to provide meals for children and families in need, so the more you play, the more you help!

  • Due Oct 13

Charity Miles

Volunteers will earn money for charity for every mile walked/ran through an app called Charity Miles. Each mile will directly be converted into donations for a charity of your choice.

  • Due Oct 13

New OAKC Remind!

As we progress more into the term and have more online service events, OAKC now has a Remind for all members to join! Use the class code @oakc2020 and join through the app, or text the code to the number 81010.
