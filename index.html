What's New

Save the Ocean

Volunteers will play the game "EARTH: Save the Ocean" on the App Store to help clean up the oceans. Hours will be self-recorded with submission of proof.

  • Due Jan 20

Color-A-Smile

Color-A-Smile is a nonprofit organization that distributes cheerful drawings to anyone in need of a smile. Volunteers will print out however many coloring pages as needed, color it with crayons or markers, and mail it to the address in the form.

  • Due Jan 21

Charity Miles

Volunteers will earn money for charity for every mile walked/ran through an app called Charity Miles. Each mile will directly be converted into donations for a charity of your choice.

  • Due Jan 20

Coming Up

CNH Foundation Scholarship

Are you a graduating senior who's been devoted to Key Club? If so, we encourage you to apply for the CNH Foundation Scholarship. Thousands of dollars are awarded each year to students who display academic excellence and service within Key Club. Consider checking it out during Winter Break and possibly even completing it! Anybody in Key Club standing may apply, so if you're a Dues-Paid member that means you're qualified to apply for it.

  • More

OAKC Dues Payment

All Key Clubbers must pay a $12 fee in order to become a dues-paid member. Pay using the ASB webstore, which is in our LinkTree under the dues payment tab. Here are the steps for payment: 1) Register for an ASB Webstore account. 2) Select Key Club under product categories. 3) Add to cart and then checkout. Also, make sure to complete the member application form in order to become a dues-paid member.
