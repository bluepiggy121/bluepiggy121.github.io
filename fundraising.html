Preferred Charities

Pediatric Trauma Program

Established by the Kiwanis CNH Foundation, PTP works to reduce the number of children who suffer from trauma by developing local projects to help support partner hospitals through donations.

United Nations Children's Fund

As a United Nations agency, UNICEF is a widespread organization that operates in over 190 countries and territories, maintaining a focus on supporting child health and nutrition as well as emergency relief.

March of Dimes

Originally founded to combat polio, March of Dimes is a non-profit organization that has shifted its focus to improving the health of mothers and babies through research on preventing infant mortality and birth defects.

Children's Miracle Network

CMN Hospitals is a non-profit organization that raises funds and awareness to combat children's health issues, distributing resources to a network of 170 children's hospitals.

Fundraising Goals

Much of our fundraising goes to the Pediatric Trauma Program, one of the four preferred charities that Oxford Academy Key Club is dedicated to. Our current fundraising goal for the Pediatric Trauma Program for the 2020-2021 term is $1,500.



So far, we have raised a total of $506 towards our goal of $1,500!
