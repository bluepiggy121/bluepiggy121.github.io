Division Event: Pixel Learn Tutoring

Volunteers will help tutor underprivileged students in topics such as SAT, ACT, and AP knowledge who cannot access private tutoring. If you have any experience in math, coding, psychology, sciences, history, languages, or the SAT/ACT, Pixel Learn is seeking more instructors who can help students with their academic questions. Remember to mention that you are from OAKC when you sign up!
